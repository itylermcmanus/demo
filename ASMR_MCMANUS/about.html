PUT ON YOUR HEADPHONES

Autonomous sensory meridian response or ASMR is a sensory experience triggered by a wide variety of sounds and characterized by pleasant tinglings throughout the head and body as well as increased feelings of relaxation. Videos that aim to trigger this response have become increasingly popular on Youtube with upwards of 1.5 million videos currently available. put on your headphones is a collection of ASMR trigger videos gathered from Youtube. However, put on your headphones does not utilizie these videos for the purpose of triggering ASMR. Alternately the videos are displayed to allow the user to manipulate groups of videos to create a variety of layered auditory experiences. The videos are initially categorized by their primary sound component and subsequently their content. These two systems of organization allow for the user to explore innumerable combinations of sounds within the collection.
As the title suggests the use of headphones is imperitive to the experience.
